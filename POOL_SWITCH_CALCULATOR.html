How These Numbers Are Calculated
gross_eff(S, P) = p × r × (S + a₀·P) / (1 + a₀)  |  p = pool performance · r = 0.000400 (live epoch rate) · a₀ = 0.3
Your epoch reward = (D / S) × (1 − m) × max(0, gross_eff − F)  |  D = your ADA · S = total pool stake · m = margin · F = fixed fee
Your annual reward = epoch_reward × 73 epochs/year
Switching cost = 2 × old_pool_epoch_reward  — Cardano takes ~2 epochs (~10 days) for new delegation to activate; your stake earns nothing during that window.
Break-even time = switching_cost / (new_epoch_reward − old_epoch_reward) epochs × 5 days/epoch
Net benefit (1 yr) = annual_gain − switching_cost  |  annual_gain = new_annual − old_annual
