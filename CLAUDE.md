## Project Summary

Pool Ranger is a Cardano staking cooperative. Members share their **staking key** (never their
spending/private key) with a cooperative administrator. The administrator delegates the
combined stake to carefully chosen pools. The administrator uses `ranger/SPO_REWARD_ANALYSIS_CHART.html` 
and `ranger/SPO_REWARD_ANALYSIS_CHART_COMPANION.md` to evalueate stake pools so the `ranger/SPO_REWARD_ANALYSIS_CHART.html`
must give acurate results.
