Multi-CIP Simulator — the parameter cube where multipools live

The whole (pledge × margin × delegation) cube, colored by an output metric. Move the three CIP levers and watch the region where splitting into many pools pays (the red habitable zone) shrink. Synthetic grid — no pool data. Companion to the single-pool CIP-187 simulator.

Guided tutorials

Cards appear on screen and arrows point at the control being explained; some sliders move by themselves. Click ▶ Play in a card to run it hands-free, or press Esc to leave the tour.

Render mode

Semi-transparent fog: opacity follows the metric, so dense color marks where the metric is large. Best first look at the field's shape.
