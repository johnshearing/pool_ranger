Reference Formulas
gross(S, P) = r × (S + a₀·P) / (1 + a₀)  |  a₀ = 0.3 (protocol constant)  |  S = P + external delegation
SPO income = F + m·(gross − F) + (P/S)·(1−m)·(gross − F)    [when gross > F; otherwise SPO takes all of gross]
Delegator ROA/yr = (1−m)·(gross − F) / S × 73 epochs × 100%
A = r·a₀·P / (1+a₀)  |  mmin = (A − F) / (r·P − F)   when A > F, else 0%  |  Ceiling: a₀/(1+a₀) ≈ 23.1%
r = 0.000548 (epoch rate — slowly declining as ADA reserve depletes)  |  Saturation Ssat ≈ 75 M ADA (2025)
